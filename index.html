About Eminem

Eminem, born Marshall Bruce Mathers III on October 17, 1972, is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. Known for his technical prowess, complex lyrics, and controversial themes, Eminem has become one of the best-selling music artists of all time and is often cited as one of the greatest rappers ever.

Rising to fame in the late 1990s, Eminem's unique style and provocative content quickly garnered both critical acclaim and public controversy. His alter ego, Slim Shady, became a cultural phenomenon, pushing the boundaries of hip-hop and popular music.
